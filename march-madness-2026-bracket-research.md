## Recommended Bracket Picks

### National Champion: Arizona

Arizona is the **safest pick** to win it all according to the majority of experts (33 out of ESPN's expert poll). Here's why:

- **Healthiest No. 1 seed** — Duke is missing Caleb Foster, Michigan is missing L.J. Cason. Arizona's deep 8-man rotation is fully healthy after 34 games.
- **No reliance on 3-point shooting** — The Wildcats are impervious to going cold from the outside because their effectiveness is not predicated on making 3-pointers.
- **Elite frontcourt** — 7-foot-2 center Motiejus Krivas anchors the paint, and five-star freshman Brayden Burries is playing like a lottery pick.
- **Travel advantage** — Teams have to trek across the country to play Arizona in the West Region.

### Final Four Picks

| Region | Pick | Why |

### Sleeper / Dark Horse Teams

- **Houston (2 seed, South)** — If they survive the first weekend, they play Sweet 16 and Elite Eight in Houston. Kingston Flemings + veteran core makes them dangerous.
- **Iowa State (2 seed, Midwest)** — Odds have been improving since bracket release. Bettors love their draw.
- **Purdue (2 seed)** — The Final Four is in Indianapolis. A hometown run could carry them to their first-ever NCAA Championship.
- **St. John's (5 seed, East)** — At +2500 odds with a 28-6 record, they offer value. Big East tournament champions.
